Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

At Horizons, we believe your location and daily habits are your business, not ours. This app is designed to provide sunrise and sunset information with maximum privacy.

1. Data Collection

  • No Personal Data: Horizons does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not collect your name, email, or contact details.
  • Local Processing: All sunrise, sunset, and lunar calculations are performed locally on your device. Your data never leaves your phone.
  • Location Data: The app requires your GPS coordinates to provide accurate times. This data stays on your device. It is never sent to a server, sold to third parties, or used for tracking.

2. Third-Party Services

  • App Store & Payments: Premium features are handled entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not have access to your credit card or billing information.
  • No Analytics or Ads: Horizons does not use third-party analytics (like Firebase) or advertising trackers. Your usage habits are private.

3. Data Retention

Because we do not collect your data, we have nothing to retain. Any locations you save within the app are stored locally and are deleted if you uninstall the app.

4. Your Rights

Since no data is collected, there is no data to request or delete. You have full control over location permissions via your iOS System Settings.
