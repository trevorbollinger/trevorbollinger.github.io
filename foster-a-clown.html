Foster a Clown

Fostering means that you are opening your heart and home to an at-risk clown by becoming the primary caregiver until they are adopted, or until the shelter has room to take the clown back into their facility. As a foster home, you are playing a critical role in keeping the doors open at the rescue so that other at-risk clowns can receive help. Fostering a clown is a special and important way of building a system of support for clowns in your community.
