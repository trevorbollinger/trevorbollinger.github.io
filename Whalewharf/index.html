Welcome to Whalewharf

Shape a coastal town across generations in a living, evolving RPG.

About Whalewharf

Whalewharf is a 2D RPG about observing a town evolve over time and shaping its outcome. Guide Whalewharf from humble fishing village to bustling port (or let it fall into ruin). Every decision, every quest, every friendship echoes through generations.

Generational Impact

Your actions have long-term impact. Watch Whalewharf grow, thrive, or decline based on your actions.

Living, Breathing World

NPCs age, families grow, and buildings evolve. Or devolve. Every playthrough tells a new story.

Meaningful Quests

Every quest shapes the town's future. No "right" answers, just consequences.

Development

Whalewharf is a solo project developed by Boli Development. It is currently in early development and I have no idea how long this will take. A lot of what you see in the screenshots is still just placeholder. If you happen to stumble across this page, and you have a question, or just want to chat, feel free to email me at tralion@boli.dev.
