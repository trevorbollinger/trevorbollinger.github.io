Whalewharf

Shape a coastal town across generations in a living, evolving RPG.

About the Game

Whalewharf is a 2D RPG about observing a town evolve over time and shaping its outcome. Every decision, every quest, and every friendship echoes through generations, determining whether your town thrives as a trade hub or falls into ruin.

Generational Impact

Your actions have long-term consequences. Watch as families grow, businesses rise and fall, and the town's character shifts based on your choices.

Living World

NPCs have their own lives, aging and passing on legacies. Buildings evolve or degrade. No two playthroughs produce the same history.

Meaningful Quests

Move beyond fetch quests. resolve disputes, set policies, and deal with natural disasters that shape the physical and social landscape.
